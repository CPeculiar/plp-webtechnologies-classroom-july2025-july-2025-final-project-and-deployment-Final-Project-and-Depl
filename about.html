About Grace Community Church

Learn more about our history, values, and community

Our Story

Founded in 1985, Grace Community Church has been a beacon of hope and faith in our community for nearly four decades. We started as a small group of believers meeting in homes and have grown into a vibrant congregation of over 500 members.

Our church is built on the foundation of God's love, grace, and the teachings of Jesus Christ. We believe in creating an inclusive environment where people from all walks of life can come together to worship, learn, and grow in their faith.

Our Core Values

Faith

We believe in the transformative power of faith in Jesus Christ and encourage spiritual growth through prayer, worship, and study.

Community

We foster a welcoming environment where everyone belongs and can build meaningful relationships with others.

Service

We are committed to serving our local community and making a positive impact through acts of love and kindness.

Growth

We encourage continuous learning and spiritual development through Bible study, mentorship, and discipleship programs.
